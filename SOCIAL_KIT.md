## Core narrative (EN) — 3 paragraphs

**Hook.** People lose funds on XRPL because a 34-character `r…` address looks “close enough.” Vanity phishing and clipboard malware exploit that.

**Solution.** Cryptographic Mosaic hashes the address into a deterministic visual mosaic (center anchor fixed for fast recognition) and a short acoustic signature. Change one character → the pattern and sound change completely. Optionally mint a self-issued Soulbound NFT (taxon `1001`) as an on-chain identity anchor.

**Ask.** Open the live app, compare two lookalike addresses in the Comparator, mint your keychain with Xaman, and verify at `/verify`. If you build a wallet: embed the mosaic before every outbound payment.
