Verify Mosaic Keychain

Check whether an XRPL address has a valid self-issued Mosaic Keychain NFT on Mainnet.

On-chain check

Paste a public address (r…). We query account_nfts for taxon 1001 where Issuer = Owner.

Enter an address and click verify.

What counts as valid?

  • Self-minted: you signed NFTokenMint in your wallet — not an unsolicited inbound NFT.
  • Taxon 1001: Mosaic Keychain convention.
  • Issuer == Owner: only your account can issue your identity NFT.
  • Non-transferable: utility anchor, not a tradable pass or airdrop.

Metadata is served from this domain: /api/nft/{address}
