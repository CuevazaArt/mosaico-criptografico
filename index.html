Cryptographic Mosaic

Turn every XRPL address into a unique visual and acoustic keychain. Detect phishing and clipboard hijacking before signing — without reading 34 characters one by one.

First time here? This tool turns XRPL addresses into unique color drawings. If someone alters the address, the drawing changes completely — so you spot scams instantly. Recommended flow: Register → Generator → Comparator

                  Adds visual markers and guides to the central glyph to facilitate counting.

What you just did

        
You minted a Soulbound NFT (taxon 1001) on XRPL Mainnet. It proves your mosaic identity on-chain — non-transferable, tied to your address forever until you burn it.

      

      

        
Your visual & acoustic keychain

        
Every XRPL address has a unique mosaic and melody. Memorize yours — you'll use it to spot phishing and clipboard swaps before signing.

        

      

      

        
Try the Comparator now

        
We will open the Comparator with your address on both sides — a perfect match. Next time, paste the destination from your wallet into slot B and compare before sending XRP.

      

      

        
Make it a daily habit

        
Before every send: open Comparator → your address in A → destination in B → green match + same melody = safe. Red mismatch = stop and investigate.
