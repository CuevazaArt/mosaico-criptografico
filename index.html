Turn every XRPL address into a unique visual and acoustic keychain. Detect phishing and clipboard hijacking before signing — without reading 34 characters one by one.

First time here? This tool turns XRPL addresses into unique color drawings. If someone alters the address, the drawing changes completely — so you spot scams instantly. Recommended flow: Register → Generator → Comparator

Register your XRPL keychain

Paste address → preview mosaic → connect Xaman → mint Soulbound NFT.

Step 1 — Paste your public XRPL address

From your Xaman app: copy your address (starts with r). Never paste your secret key.

Step 2 — Meet your visual keychain

This pattern is unique to your address. Memorize 2–3 details (colors, central shape, corners).

Step 3 — Connect Xaman on Mainnet

Install Xaman on your phone. Ensure enough free XRP for the NFT owner reserve (shown before mint).

Step 4 — Mint your Soulbound NFT

Your NFT URI anchors a keychain package: visual mosaic (SVG) + acoustic signature (WAV) + metadata manifest. Sign in Xaman when prompted.

⚠️ Xaman “scam” warning after mint?

        
If you just minted your keychain and Xaman flags the NFT as suspicious, it is often a false positive — not a failed mint. Confirm taxon 1001, Issuer = Owner = your address, and ✅ Registered in Comparator.

What you just did

          
You minted a Soulbound NFT (taxon 1001) on XRPL Mainnet. It proves your mosaic identity on-chain — non-transferable, tied to your address until you burn it.

        

        

          
Your visual & acoustic keychain

          
Every XRPL address has a unique mosaic and melody. Memorize yours — you'll use it to spot phishing and clipboard swaps before signing.

          

            

            Tap your mosaic to open the Comparator (window stays open)
          

        

        

          
Try the Comparator

          
On the last step you'll open the Comparator with your address on both sides — a perfect match. Next time, paste the destination from your wallet into slot B before sending XRP.

        

        

          
Make it a daily habit

          
Before every send: Comparator → your address in A → destination in B → green match + same melody = safe. Red mismatch = stop and investigate. When ready, click Close window.
